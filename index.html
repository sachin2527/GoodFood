WHY IS PREVENTION OF FOOD WASTE IMPORTANT

1.3 metric gigatons of edible food is wasted every year and at least 795 million people are undernourished worldwide. More than a third of all the food that’s produced on our planet never reaches a table.

WHAT IS GOODFOOD ?

GoodFood feeds the Poor ,we connects restaurants and charities in India. so that restaurants with excess food can easily post their leftovers Once these leftovers are posted, nearby charities and food pantries are notified of the posting via our Website.

Our Objectives

  • Connect individuals and businesses with local organizations that collect and redistribute food to those in need.
  • Educate the public on the environmental, social, and economic impacts of food waste.
